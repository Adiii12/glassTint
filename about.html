About GLASS & TINT

Building trust through quality service, expert craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction since 2009.

Our Story

Founded in 2009 by automotive enthusiast Mike Thompson, TintPro began as a small operation with a simple mission: provide the highest quality window tinting and auto glass services with unmatched customer care.

What started in a single-bay garage has grown into the region's most trusted name in automotive glass services. Our success is built on three core principles: quality materials, expert installation, and standing behind our work with comprehensive warranties.

Today, we serve thousands of satisfied customers annually, from individual car owners to large commercial fleets. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us the preferred choice for discerning customers who demand the best.

Our Mission

"To provide exceptional window tinting and auto glass services that enhance our customers' comfort, safety, and satisfaction while building lasting relationships based on trust and quality."
